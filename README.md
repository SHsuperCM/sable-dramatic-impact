Hey so you know how when you crash your ship it just stops on the ground?

I wanted crashing to feel more immersive but the only option available was true impact, but then true impact actually breaks your plane!

So what if instead the damage was only visual?

# Sable: Dramatic Impact

Sable: Dramatic Impact adds cosmetic explosions whenever your ship crashes! All effects are configurable, there is compat with CBC smoke, your blocks wont break, GUARANTEED!

![Crashed plane](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/8934ffca22e4fd18e245f8b4f3ec117746a95a30_0.webp)

### Known issues:

*   The conditions for impacts are not perfect, sometimes it triggers when it shouldnt and sometimes it doesnt trigger enough. Still thinking about how to solve that
*   Collisions are only between physics objects, create bearings for propellors and flaps dont cause explosions. Will look into fixing that.
